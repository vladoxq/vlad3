Kyiv Zoo is one of the oldest and largest zoos in Ukraine. It begins its history in 1909. At the beginning, there were only 17 species of pets in the zoo. Today, about 400 species of animals live here. And, despite the difficult war years and times of perestroika, the zoo still works and delights tourisппts and residents of Kyiv.

The zoo is very well located. The topography of the area is suitable for different types of animals. There are plains and hills here. Artificial reservoirs and ponds appeared. A huge park was also planted. Here you can observe not only animals, but also admire the beauty of nature.

Of course, this place conquers children the most. Young visitors are happy to meet exotic animals: crocodiles, elephants, rhinoceroses and others. The opportunity to pet and feed funny pets causes special admiration for children. At the zoo, you can have plenty of laughs while watching the funny monkeys.

Adults will not be bored either. In the zoo, you can observe animals nearby, which were previously seen only on the TV screen. For example, the department with bears that sit not in cages, but behind glass. It seems that nothing separates you from the animals and you are in their usual environment. The zoo also has animals listed in the Red Book. For example, the Kyiv Zoo has a black swan. You can watch this graceful and beautiful bird for hours.

For those who visit the zoo for the first time, the services of a tour guide are provided. The staff is interested in ensuring that every visitor leaves them satisfied. They also make sure that all zoo residents feel at home. All animals are well-groomed and mobile. The price of an adult ticket is 50 hryvnias. A walk through the zoo will be the most unforgettable adventure for both adults and children.
